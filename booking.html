BOOKING

To schedule a lesson or presentation, or for questions contact me at j_walter@msn.com or by phone at 303-981-8116.

Lessons are given on Tuesdays and Sundays. Lesson content, location and what to bring will be discussed by phone or e-mail prior to the appointment.

Lessons missed by the student without 24 hour notice will be charged the full rate for the time booked. This includes each member of any group lesson: if a lesson is booked for three people and only 2 show the total charge remains as for 3 people. Re-scheduling missed lessons are done at my discretion.

Lessons cancelled with 24 hour notice may of course be re-scheduled. If I miss your lesson without 24 hour notice you will receive a refund and a free lesson.
